# Sample Document for RAG Portfolio

This is a sample text document. You can add PDF, DOCX, or TXT files to the data/ folder.

## What is RAG?

RAG stands for Retrieval-Augmented Generation. It combines:
1. A vector database (embeddings of your documents)
2. An LLM (like GPT) to generate answers
3. Real-time indexing — when you add new files, they are automatically indexed

## How to use this app

1. Add your documents to the data/ folder
2. Wait for indexing (check the sidebar)
3. Ask questions in natural language
4. The app will find relevant chunks and generate answers

The Pathway framework handles all the complexity: parsing, chunking, embedding, and incremental updates.
